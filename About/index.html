About CitiTek

CitiTek is a city-building strategy game where players take on the role of a futuristic urban planner, managing resources, expanding infrastructure, and keeping citizens happy in a high-tech world. Inspired by classic city simulation games, CitiTek adds its own twist with streamlined mechanics, a sleek pixel-art style, and strategic depth.

Features

  • Plan Your City: Plan and construct residential, commercial, and industrial zones.
  • Manage Systems: Balance the supply and demand of your cities needs.
  • Hilarious NPCs: Talk to the NPCs designed to help you, all with their own personalities.
  • Pretty Ary: Stylish retro visuals with crisp tile-based graphics.
  • Great Music: A great mix of sollem jazz, and upbeat beats too which immerse you in your city.
